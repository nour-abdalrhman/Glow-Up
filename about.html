The Founder

A girl with a dream
and a makeup bag

"I couldn't find products that felt made for me — so I made them myself."

It started in a small room with too many lipsticks and a deep love for beauty. I was tired of products that promised the world but didn't deliver — especially for women like me who wanted quality without compromise.

So I spent two years researching, testing, and falling in love with formulas that actually worked. Glow Up was born out of that obsession — a brand that celebrates every skin tone, every skin type, and every version of beautiful.

Today, Glow Up is home to 500+ products, 48,000 happy customers, and a community of women who believe that beauty is personal, powerful, and always worthy of investment.

Nour Mohammed
Founder & CEO, Glow Up

How it started

Our Journey So Far

2020
The Spark 🌸
Glow Up was born in a small apartment with 12 lipstick shades and a big dream. Our first Instagram post got 200 saves overnight.
2021
First 1,000 Customers 💕
We launched our skincare line and hit 1,000 orders in our first month. The community was growing and so were we.
2022
Going Fully Vegan
We reformulated our entire line to be 100% vegan and cruelty-free. Because beauty shouldn't cost the earth its kindness.
2023
10,000 Happy Girls 🎉
We celebrated our 10,000th order with a giveaway that crashed our website. The Glow Up family was officially a movement.
2025
500 Products & Beyond 🚀
Today, Glow Up has 500+ products, 48,000 customers, and is still growing — one gorgeous woman at a time.
